Here's all the stuff I do.

Colors 4 Causes was started by Alexandra H., age 7 in the Spring of 2018.

Personal requests.

Patrons have the opportunity to choose an item to be created specifically on request, choose their colors and choose from one of 4 featured causes, to which 25% of proceeds will be donated. Each month will feature four new causes, so please keep coming back!

Brightening the future.

Alex thanks you all for supporting the things that are dear to her. She looks forward to a bright future creating for you and benefiting those less fortunate.

Helping others

By Combining her passion for art and her acute awareness of the need to help others, her vision is to create one-of-a-kind pieces that people can feel good about wearing, not only because they are unique, but because they benefit those in need.

Lets make the world a brighter and colorful place, Go on wear it!
